# AudioGrabber

Convert videos to MP3 in one click.

## How to Use

1. **Download** `AudioGrabber.exe` from the [Releases](../../releases) page or [Actions artifacts](../../actions/workflows/build-windows.yml).
2. **Double-click** `AudioGrabber.exe` to start the app (no installation needed).
3. **Select videos** — click "Pick Videos" or drag-and-drop video files into the app.
4. **Extract** — click "Start" and wait for the MP3s to appear in the "MP3" folder next to your videos.

That's it! Your original videos remain untouched.
